Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform, features, and how we can help you.

How does the AI analyze my resume?

Our AI uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) and machine learning models trained on vast datasets of job descriptions and successful resumes. It identifies keywords, skills, action verbs, and patterns to provide comprehensive feedback and suggestions.

Is my personal data and resume information kept private?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and data security. All data is encrypted, and we adhere to strict privacy policies. Your resume content is only used to provide you with our services and is not shared with third parties without your consent.

What types of files can I upload for resume analysis?

Currently, we support PDF (.pdf) and Microsoft Word (.docx) formats for direct upload. You can also paste your resume text directly into the provided text area.

How accurate is the job role prediction?

Our AI models are continuously trained to provide highly accurate job role predictions based on your resume's content and skills. While it's a powerful guide, it's always good to consider it alongside your own research and preferences.

Can this platform help if I'm looking to change careers?

Yes! Our skill gap analysis and career roadmap features are particularly helpful for career changers. We can help you identify transferable skills and what new skills you might need to acquire for your desired field.
